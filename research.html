Research

in machine learning and optimization for radiotherapy
My research focuses on embedding outcome prediction models into radiotherapy optimization, especially machine learning models. I'm developing pyanno4rt, an open-source Python library for radiotherapy treatment plan optimization, and seamaze, an open-source Python library for classical and dynamical low-rank CMA-ES. https://orcid.org/0009-0005-6603-3088

Karlsruhe Institute of Technology (KIT), Computational Science and Mathematical Methods (CSMM), PhD Student
Supervisor: Martin Frank

German Cancer Research Center (DKFZ), Radiotherapy Optimization, Associated PhD student
Supervisors: Oliver Jäkel, Niklas Wahl
